HomeLock Smart

Time-based control for your HomeKit smart home.

Support Privacy

What it does

HomeLock turns your HomeKit-compatible outlets, switches, and lights into time-managed devices. Set bedtime cutoffs for the gaming console. Limit screen time on the TV. Cut off WiFi at midnight. Sync with your spouse's iPhone via iCloud Family Sharing.

Features

⏱ Time-based locking

Lock any outlet, switch, or light for minutes, hours, or until a specific time.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family sync

Locks set on your iPhone instantly sync to your spouse's via iCloud Family Sharing.

🔐 Face ID + PIN

The app itself is protected so kids can't simply open it and unlock the TV.

🗣 Siri Shortcuts

"Hey Siri, lock the TV for two hours."

📅 Schedules

Recurring lockouts for weekday bedtimes and weekend curfews.

🔒 Privacy-first

No servers, no tracking. Everything in your private iCloud.
